Accent Colors

Orange is the primary accent; blue and green cycle in as secondary and tertiary accents.

Orange #d97757 Blue #6a9bcc Green #788c5d

Component Cards

Each accent color is used as a left-border highlight on a tinted background.

Orange Accent

Primary accent — draws attention to the most important action or callout on the page.

Blue Accent

Secondary accent — used for informational elements, links, and supporting highlights.

Green Accent

Tertiary accent — ideal for success states, confirmations, or nature-adjacent content.

Dark Background Usage

When the background is Dark (#141413), body copy flips to Light (#faf9f5) and secondary text uses Mid Gray (#b0aea5). Accent colors remain unchanged — they read well on dark surfaces.

Buttons

Three variants to cover primary CTAs, secondary actions, and ghost/outline patterns.
